Transaction process

We will professionally and privately process your request. Your transaction should be finished within 12 hours. In rare cases, it may take up to 3 days.

You will receive an email with the proposed amount of Duino-Coins you will receive.
If you'll accept exchange at the proposed price, after sending the prediscussed amount of Coin Magi to address you'll receive, desired Duino-Coins will be added to your Duino-Coin wallet address.

You can then e-mail us back and review this exchange (transaction speed, clarity, etc.)
